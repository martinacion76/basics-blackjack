Hello! Welcome to Blackjack!

You and the dealer both get two cards at the start. You can then choose to get another card to add to your deck (hit) or stick to your current cards (stand).

When you choose to stand, it becomes the dealer's turn. Then, the winner is chosen.

The player with cards of a sum closest to 21 wins, but going over 21 is not considered.

Click deal to start the game. Good luck!
